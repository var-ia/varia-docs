# Boundary

**Varia does not decide what is true. Varia makes visible how public knowledge changes.**

## What Varia is

- A deterministic observation engine
- An audit trail for revision histories
- A framework for extracting structured evidence from unstructured diffs
- A platform for evaluating model-assisted interpretations against ground truth

## What Varia is not

- A fact-checker or truth-verification system
- A moderation or content policing tool
- A predictive model for future edits
- A judgment engine for healthcare, finance, or legal decisions
- A replacement for domain expertise

## Open source

**What changed?** — Everything Varia produces is auditable, reproducible, and inspectable.

**Not included** — Domain-specific decision judgment. Varia observes change. It does not tell you what to do about it.

## The line

Varia stops at the boundary between observability and judgment. If a tool needs to decide whether a change is *good*, *bad*, *true*, or *false*, that tool lives outside Varia. Varia answers only: "What changed?"
